A Different Kind of Sweet

We are a family owned business serving Peruvian desserts. We make all our desserts using fresh, authentic ingredients, such as purple corn. In combining the familiar flavors of cinnamon, cloves, lemon, caramel, and squash, we've crafted a dessert menu that represents the culture and hospitality of the Peruvian people. We invite you to visit our truck to taste true Peruvian flavors and see why we say Dulce is "A Different Kind of Sweet."
